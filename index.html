Linea Closer To The Moon

            Linea Closer To The Moon restaurant offers a dining experience that garners appreciation for its cozy ambiance and live music,
             although it can get quite noisy and crowded at times. Guests have called out the tastefulness and visual appeal of the dishes,
            yet some caution that the portion sizes may disappoint those looking for value, given the price.
            The service at this establishment earns rave reviews for its friendliness and attentiveness, but there have been occasional mentions of disorganization and delays.
            overall, the atmosphere and service are highlights, while opinions on food consistency and value vary among visitors.
